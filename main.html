Glass

Voiceover

Glass is made from melted sand. It flows, but we call it solid.
We built windows to keep out weather, then forgot the weather was part of the system.
When we learned to make clear glass, we called it vision. But seeing is not the same as understanding.

The Known

Silica. Transparent. Shatters clean. Melting point: 1700°C.
Used for sight, containment, insulation, control.

The Misunderstanding

Glass gave us the illusion of clarity.
But it reflects more than it reveals.

What Was Forgotten

Glass was once a barrier between realms — used in ritual, symbol, containment.
Now it is everywhere, unconsidered. It hides its fragility in modernity’s surface.
