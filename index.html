We live in an age that claims to understand the world through replication. We build machines that learn like brains. We model memory on mycelium. We recreate photosynthesis in panels, and DNA in code.

This is not deference. It is mimicry without context.

It borrows from biological and ecological intelligence while erasing the cultural systems that have lived symbiotically with nature for millennia. Nature becomes interface. Knowledge becomes product.

Can we ever understand a system we are inside of?

This is not a warning. It is a record of our confidence.
